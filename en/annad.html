About me

I am born and raised in Ísafjörður, a small rural town in the Westfjords of Iceland. I'm married with two daughters born in 2007 and 2009. My wife is an artist from Serbia and one of our biggest dreams is to have a house in the countryside and live off the land and do what we love, ie program, make music and art.

I have always been a hard worker and a complete finisher. I'm a really quick learner and don't need long to adapt to new environments. A good example of that would be a music programming course (Max/MSP) I attended at the art academy, before I had any programming knowledge at all. The teacher of that course had me tutor the other students at the end of that course, I had gotten such a good hold of Max (the result of the course was a program I named Le Grande, which can be found here).
I am also very stubborn and don't give up if I hit walls. I am, though, quite realistic as well, so I can very well realize if the wall is worth breaking through.
